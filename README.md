# Online Retail Sales Dashboard  
E-Commerce Data Analysis Project | Power BI  

---

## Project Overview  

This project presents an end-to-end analysis of online retail transaction data to evaluate revenue performance, customer contribution, product demand, and geographic sales distribution. The objective is to transform transactional data into structured business insights and communicate those insights through an interactive Power BI dashboard.

---

## Dataset  

The dataset used for this project can be downloaded below:

[Download Online Retail Dataset](./Online Retail Data Set.xlsx)


---

## Business Questions  

- What is the total revenue generated during the selected period?  
- How many distinct customers contributed to total revenue?  
- What is the total quantity of products sold?  
- Which countries generate the highest revenue?  
- Who are the top customers by revenue contribution?  
- Which products are sold in the highest quantities?  
- How does revenue trend month-over-month?  

---

## Data Preparation  

The following preprocessing steps were performed:

- Removed cancelled transactions  
- Handled missing Customer IDs  
- Created Revenue column (Quantity × Unit Price)  
- Extracted Month and Year from Invoice Date  
- Structured date hierarchy for trend analysis  

---

## Dashboard Development (Power BI)  

### Core KPIs  

- Distinct Customers  
- Total Revenue  
- Total Quantity Sold  
- Average Revenue per Customer  

### Analytical Visualizations  

- Monthly Revenue Trend  
- Revenue by Country  
- Top 10 Customers by Revenue  
- Top 10 Products Sold  
- Dynamic Date Range Filter  

---

## Key Insights  

- Revenue peaks during the final quarter, indicating seasonal demand.  
- A limited number of countries contribute a majority of total revenue.  
- Revenue concentration is high among a small group of customers.  
- Product demand is concentrated among specific high-performing products.  

---

## Business Implications  

- Focus marketing efforts on high-revenue countries.  
- Strengthen retention strategies for high-value customers.  
- Optimize inventory for best-selling products.  
- Leverage seasonal patterns for promotional planning.
